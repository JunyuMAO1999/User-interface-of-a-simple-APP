User interface of a simple APP


Brief introduction

Arabic language is famous for its complicated numeral-noun construction. You can refer to this link for details about numeral-noun construction and this link for details about intricate but also intriguing Arabic grammar.

Since I was then commissary in charge of studies in my class and I had the responsibility to help others practice this complicated numeral-noun construction with ease, I designed a VB-based APP in Grade 4, where the users can do exercises of Arabic Numeral Phrases.

Here comes the APP!~

This is a simple and easy-use application designed by VB, Visual Basic. As you can see, the icon of the APP is as simple as its function. [Lol]

The filename is written in Chinese and it means "Numeral Phrases interpreting exercises assistant for economic and financial texts" in English.



Click on it, and you will see this interface. So middle-eastern it is! [Lol]

The title means "Numeral Phrases interpreting exercises for economic and financial texts".

The button means "Please begin your show". [Lol] (Actually, this was once a piece of internet catchphrases in China.)

The text in the right textbox means "To generate a NOUN in random".

The text in the left textbox means "To generate a NUMBER over 10 thousand in random". (from 10 thousand to 1 million/ 1 billion/ 10 billion/ 100 billion/ 1 trillion respectively)



Click on the button, and you will see the result!

WOW! Simple and easy!



So the users can get the numbers in random and their partner -- the nouns. (In Chinese, there are also measure words preceding the nouns.)

Every measure word comprises ONE character, while every noun comprises TWO characters.

For example, for the first line, “个” is the measure word and “台灯” is the noun which means "table lamp".
